# 🚗 Garage

**Garage** is an intuitive, offline-first vehicle maintenance assistant designed to help you keep your personal fleet in top shape. Whether you're tracking oil changes, monitoring maintenance schedules, or logging service history, Garage provides a clean, modern interface to manage everything.

Built with performance and reliability in mind, Garage is designed for users who want to stay organized without the hassle of manual spreadsheets or paper records.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **Complete Vehicle Management**: Track your fleet with ease. View recent service history, monitor odometer readings, and keep all your vehicle details in one place.
* **Offline-First Reliability**: Your data stays with you. Even without an internet connection, you can log services, update vehicle info, and view records. Everything syncs seamlessly to the cloud the moment you're back online.
* **Smart Maintenance Insights**: Get intelligent suggestions for upcoming maintenance tasks. Never miss an important service interval again.
* **Clean & Simple**: Designed with modern Material 3 design principles, Garage offers a focused, uncluttered experience.
* **Secure & Personal**: Your vehicle data is protected by industry-standard Firebase authentication, ensuring only you have access to your records.

---

## 🏗️ Built for Stability

Garage leverages the latest in Android technology to ensure a smooth, professional experience:
* **Jetpack Compose**: For a fluid, responsive, and modern UI.
* **Offline-First Architecture**: Your records are stored locally using Room, meaning the app is always fast and works anywhere.
* **Firebase Integration**: Secure cloud synchronization and authentication.
